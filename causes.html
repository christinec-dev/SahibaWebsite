Our Causes

Our Ongoing Causes

COVID-19 Emergency

With the detremental rise of COVID-19 infections, it is no surprise that there has been a spike in unemployment, death and stress amongst our communities. Daily, we are flooded with request for help and support, but we alone cannot provide for the masses, and therefore we are asking you for help.

Any donations will go towards the feeding, transportation, toiletry provison & support of women in need.

Donate Now

Weekly Soup Kitchen

Every Weekend, people wait in line to receive the first proper meal they've had all week, but with the numbers increasing weekly, we do not have the resources or capacity to feed all of them.

We would like to expand our feeding potential, and have the kitchen twice a week. If businesses and other individuals come on board with us we will be able to help more women and starving members of the community.

Donate Now

Mandela Day

This Mandela Day we are doing our 67 minutes, and to do so we have identified 67 families and 30 orphans to donate food, clothing and blanket parcels towards. We already have the support of companies like KPMG, Albany Bread Johannesburg, as well as the contrinutions of individuals that have been our sponsors.

We welcome all individuals and businesses to take part in this fundraiser, and feed a family this Mandela Day.

Want To Volunteer?

Become a weekly volunteer by helping us gather food parcels and feed women & families in poverty, healthcare, disaster relief as well as fundraising tasks, adiministration & environmental cleanup.
