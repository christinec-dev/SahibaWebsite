We need donations and weekly volunteers

Sahiba Foundation receives no government funding, and thus we are calling out to all those who are willing to dedicate their time, talents and financial and physical support towards bettering vulnerable, disabled and less-fortuned woman through sustainable programs and empowerment.

Our aim is to build bridges of hope and help.

Sahiba Foundation was founded in Johannesburg, South Africa in 2019 with the simple task of providing assistance to women & young girls who are in need - especially the physically and mentally challenged, impoverished and abused woman across all communities in South Africa.

We do this by providing them with the necessary skills and tools built by our sustainable programs, as well as focusing on their safety and health.

Know More

How We Empower Women

Emotional Support

We organize and pay for specialized psychological help or social workers to assist women who have reached out to us, and has gone through detremental abuse or mental trauma.

Basic Need Provision

When women in need reach us, or we do community outreach projects, we find the money, helping hands and assistance to obtain food, clothing and toiletries for those in need.

Free Skills Training

We help & motivate children in orphanages by teaching them English, and provide both young and old women with the necessary life skills so they are better equipped for life.
