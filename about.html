About Sahiba Foundation

Our aim is to build bridges of hope and help.

Realizing the high rise of Gender Based Violence in South Africa, Sahiba Foundation was established in Johannesburg, South Africa in 2019 with the simple task of providing assistance to women & young girls who are in need - especially the physically and mentally challenged, impoverished and abused woman across all communities in South Africa.

Our team currently consists of 10 people, which includes our founding members Mr Thubelihle Zooma, Talent Masondo and Chantelle Fourie, as well as seven permanent volunteers.

How To Get Help From Us

Women who need assistance are encouraged to contact Sahiba Foundation through our Contact Form, or via a message on our social media platforms such as Faceboon, Instagram, and LinkedIn. If you know of a woman who might be in need, don't hesistate to inform us and we will reach out towards the respected member.

If we are not able to assist these women, then we will refer them to an organization that can.

How We Empower Women

Emotional Support

We organize and pay for specialized psychological help or social workers to assist women who have reached out to us, and has gone through detremental abuse or mental trauma.

Basic Need Provision

When women in need reach us, or we do community outreach projects, we find the money, helping hands and assistance to obtain food, clothing and toiletries for those in need.

Free Skills Training

We help & motivate children in orphanages by teaching them English, and provide both young and old women with the necessary life skills so they are better equipped for life.

Want To Volunteer?

Become a weekly volunteer by helping us gather food parcels and feed women & families in poverty, healthcare, disaster relief as well as fundraising tasks, adiministration & environmental cleanup.

Sahiba Foundation embraces, empowers and supports all women, especially those who were abused, vulnerable and mentally and physically challenged.
